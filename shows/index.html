About the Film

This Is Not A Draft follows Post-9/11 veterans as they navigate life after service — the invisible battles, the slow reckonings, the moments of grace. Directed by Jack Blair and produced through Happy Jack Media, the film spent years in the making, filming across the country to capture stories that rarely make it to the screen.

Selected for the Florence International Film Festival, where it earned two awards, the documentary is now available to watch free on multiple platforms. No subscription required.
