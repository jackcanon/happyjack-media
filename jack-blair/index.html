Jack Blair

Director  ·  Happy Jack Media

Jack Blair is an independent filmmaker and the founder of Happy Jack Media. His work focuses on veterans, communities, and the human stories that tend to get left out of the larger narrative — the ones that deserve to be told carefully, and remembered.

His debut documentary, This Is Not A Draft, follows Post-9/11 veterans navigating life after service. The film has screened at festivals including the Florence International Film Festival and earned two awards. It is now available free on YouTube, Fawesome TV, and Relay.

Jack is based in the United States and continues to develop new projects through Happy Jack Media. He also covers stories in sports — most recently, the 2026 FIFA World Cup.
