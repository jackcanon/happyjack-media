Who We Are

Arizona's Central Hub for Veteran Connection, Care & Community

The AZ Veterans Resource Center is founded and led by veterans with one powerful mission: to ensure no veteran or family stands alone in Arizona.

We provide trauma-informed case management, peer support, and holistic wellness programs that restore purpose, stability, and belonging — meeting veterans where they are and walking alongside them every step of the way.

Our vision is a connected Arizona where every veteran and family thrives — grounded in wisdom, strengthened by courage, guided by justice, balanced by temperance, and driven by purpose.

What We Offer

Programs & Services

Comprehensive, veteran-centered support designed to address the full spectrum of need — from practical assistance to long-term wellness.

Case Management

One-on-one guidance connecting veterans to the right resources, benefits, and support networks.

Housing & Employment

Pathways to stable homes and meaningful careers — because security is the foundation of everything.

Wellness & Healing

Holistic care programs that restore balance, address trauma, and support lasting mental and physical health.

Family & Peer Support

Strengthening families and building peer bonds — because healing happens best in community.

Community Events

Regular events that build connection, foster camaraderie, and remind every veteran they belong here.

The Foundation of Our Work

The Stoic Virtues in Action

Ancient wisdom applied to modern service — guiding every program, every conversation, and every decision we make.

I

Courage

Facing adversity with conviction — and helping veterans do the same.

II

Temperance

Practicing balance and calm in all we do, personally and as an organization.

III

Justice

Serving every veteran with fairness, compassion, and dignity — no exceptions.

IV

Wisdom

Learning through reflection and the shared experience of those who served.

V

Purpose

Living and leading with intention — and helping veterans rediscover their own.

Real Impact. Real Change.

Since 2023, AZVRC has expanded its reach, deepened community partnerships, and redefined the veteran experience in Arizona. With a growing network of allied organizations, we're helping more veterans reconnect with purpose — and rise stronger than ever.
