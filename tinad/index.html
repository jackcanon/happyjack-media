About The Film

They Answered
The Call

When tragedy struck on September 11th, these Veterans chose to serve. This Is Not A Draft captures their stories — the sacrifices made, the battles fought far from home, and the lives they've built long after the war ended.

Inspired by the Veteran Stories Podcast, this documentary hopes that by sharing these stories, others will be inspired to serve in their own ways. Every story matters, and each person can make a difference.
